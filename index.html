Hi! This is a website created to display the fan art revolving around the new Gen 8 Pokémon games that are releasing later 2019. If you aren't a die-hard Pokémon fan no worries! I hope you can still appreciate the fan art on the starters.

If you are new: here's the gist of it. Grass type Pokémon are weak to Fire types. Fire types are weak to Water types. And you guessed it! Water types are weak to Grass types. There are many more "types" of Pokémon in the Pokéverse, but for now, that's all that you need to know until more generation-specific Pokémon are released.

Under the "Fan Art" tab, I included some cool hypothetical drawings of the starter evolutions. The links are placed below each image for you to check out yourself.

The "About" tab simply features a little information about me, the creator, if you're interested.

Overall, most of the images used are not mine and are cited either directly underneath the images provided or provided on top of the code. None belong to me and all rights of the images remain with Nintendo and the Pokémon Company.
